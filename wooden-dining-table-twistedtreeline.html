    Inspired by the sweeping natural curves of the Baobab tree, this central pedestal design maximizes seating space around the table with a beautiful symmetry that makes it a functional contemporary sculpture. This table is part of the Elements Collection which is a collaboration between Korean steel artist Alex Joo and Abacus, inspired by nature's shapes and forms, married with materials and craftsmanship of the finest quality.

    Every curved panel of this multi-dimensional bases must be cut to millimeter perfection. All of the work can only be done by hand, welding the structure using a painstaking process before the full length of every single seam is polished on both faces. A durable powder-coated finish connects the flowing curves with color and creates the contrasting shadows that make these bases something incredibly special in the flesh.
